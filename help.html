Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Most orders ship within 24–48 hours. Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days depending on your location.

What is the return policy?

You can return items within 7 days of delivery in original condition. Refunds are processed within 3–5 days.

How do I apply coupons?

Use SMART10, SMART20 or SMART30 on the Cart page. Discounts apply to subtotal.
